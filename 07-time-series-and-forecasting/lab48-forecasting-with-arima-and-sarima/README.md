# 🧪 Lab 48: Forecasting with ARIMA and SARIMA

## 📌 Lab Overview

This lab focused on understand the fundamentals of arima and sarima models for time series forecasting through a hands-on Google Colab workflow centered on **Forecasting with ARIMA and SARIMA**.

## 🎯 Objective

- Understand the fundamentals of ARIMA and SARIMA models for time series forecasting
- Apply the Augmented Dickey-Fuller (ADF) test to check for stationarity in time series data
- Use auto_arima for automatic parameter selection and manual tuning for ARIMA models
- Build and evaluate ARIMA and SARIMA models for forecasting
- Generate future predictions with confidence intervals
- Visualize forecasting results and interpret model performance
