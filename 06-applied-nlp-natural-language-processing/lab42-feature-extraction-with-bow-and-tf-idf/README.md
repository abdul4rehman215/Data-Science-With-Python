# 🧪 Lab 42: Feature Extraction with BoW and TF-IDF

## 📌 Lab Overview

This lab focused on understand the concepts of bag of words (bow) and term frequency-inverse document frequency (tf-idf) through a hands-on Google Colab workflow centered on **Feature Extraction with BoW and TF-IDF**.

- Understand the concepts of Bag of Words (BoW) and Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF)
